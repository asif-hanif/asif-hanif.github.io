I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Computer Vision Department at the Mohamed Bin Zayed University of Artificial Intelligence (MBZUAI). My research focuses on the intersection of multimodal deep learning and trustworthy AI. I received my PhD in Computer Vision from MBZUAI in 2025 and I was kindly advised by Prof. Fahad Khan and Prof. Salman Khan. Before joining MBZUAI, I was a Research Associate at the ITU. I received my B.S. and M.S. degrees in Electrical Engineering from ITU and UET, respectively.

Research

Trustworthy AI

My most of the research focuses on the development of trustworthy AI techniques to ensure the safety and reliability of AI systems.

Adversarial Robustness · Backdoor Attacks · Multimodal Learning · Vision-Language Models · Audio-Language Models

Test-Time Adaptation & Prompt Learning

I am also interested in the development of test-time adaptation and prompt learning techniques to improve the performance of AI systems at test time.

Vision-Language Models · Audio-Language Models · Visual Prompt Learning · Audio Prompt Learning
